The Reddest Red

An investigation into why the color red (#FF0000) shows up differently on Figma vs. code. It all has to do with color spaces.

Does your browser support wide gamut display P3 colors? YESNO.

Note that even if your browser supports P3 colors, your monitor might not. If you can't see a difference, try using your laptop instead.
